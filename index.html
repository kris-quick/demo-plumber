About Us

A Manchester Team Who Actually Show Up

City Plumbing was started in 2009 by Dave Halloran with one simple idea: give Manchester homeowners a plumber they can genuinely rely on. No call centres, no subcontractors, no surprise bills.

Over 15 years later we've built our reputation entirely on word of mouth. Most of our work still comes from referrals and customers who've been with us for years.

  • Upfront pricing before we start — no hidden extras
